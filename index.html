A note I mean with my whole heart

My dearest Gudani,

I made this for the moments when you need to remember just how loved you are. You bring warmth into my life in ways I could never fully explain, in your laugh, your softness, your strength, and all the small things that make you completely you.

I also want to say I am truly sorry for how I have been treating you this past week. You did not deserve to feel hurt, ignored, or like you were anything less than important to me. I know my actions have not reflected how much I care about you, and I take responsibility for that.

I do not want my pride, moods, or mistakes to make you question your place in my life. You mean so much to me, and I am sorry for the moments I was not gentle with your heart when I should have been.

Thank you for being a part of my days. Thank you for the memories we already have, and for every little memory we have not made yet. I do not need a perfect life; I just want a life where I keep finding my way back to you — and becoming better for you, not just apologising with words.

Even on the ordinary days, you are still my favourite part.

Always yours,
